Each recharge is split across calendar months in proportion to the days until the next recharge, assuming roughly even daily use. The latest balance is marked pending since it may not be fully consumed yet, and is excluded from the average.
Note: a BPDB recharge isn't pure energy — it also covers the monthly demand charge, meter rent, 5% VAT and any rebate, so these figures reflect cash spent over time, not kWh.
